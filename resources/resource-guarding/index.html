Free Dog Training Tips

Resource guarding is a safety-aware problem.

Guarding food, toys, space, people, or stolen objects can stay mild, or it can become dangerous. The best next step depends on severity and bite risk.

Where to start

Do not grab items out of your dog's mouth, punish warning signs, or force conflict around resources. Reduce rehearsal, create safer setups, and get help if there is risk.

Best next step

If there is bite risk, escalation, kids in the home, or you feel unsafe, book a consult.
