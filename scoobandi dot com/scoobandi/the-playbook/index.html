What the 8 weeks look like

  1. Week 1

    STOP IT!

    Learn how to organize life together with your dog so you can stop the pain of problem behaviors today.

  2. Week 2

    Playing with your food

    Discover the game I use to teach almost everything — and why your dog will actually want to work with you.

  3. Week 3

    Your time My time

    Decompression walks, mental boundaries, and how to flip the switch from free time to focused.

  4. Week 4

    Obedience that actually works when you need it to

    Sit, come, down, stay, middle, place — built the right way so they hold up in real life, not just in your living room.

  5. Week 5

    Leash walking

    How to use play to get a loose leash walk — without a battle on every block.

  6. Week 6

    Make it happen, make it useful

    What to do when your dog doesn't do what you ask, and how to use your obedience when it actually matters.

  7. Week 7

    No

    Using punishment kindly and effectively to eradicate the behaviors that aren't going away on their own.

  8. Week 8

    Your dog, your life, your plan

    Lock in a plan that holds up after the class ends — for your dog, your home, and your real life.
