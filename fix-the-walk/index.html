The problem

If walks make you tense before you even grab the leash, this is for you.

Maybe your dog pulls like a freight train. Maybe they explode when they see another dog.

Maybe they bark, lunge, spin, drag you, scan the environment, ignore you, or make you feel like every neighbor is watching.

And maybe you have tried being calmer, using more treats, watching videos, buying better gear, switching tools, practicing commands, or hoping they would grow out of it.

But the walk is still the thing that makes your day harder.

That is the problem Fix the Walk is built for.

The goal

The goal is not a perfect heel.

The goal is being able to walk your dog without feeling embarrassed, braced, or trapped.

  • A dog who can pay attention outside.
  • A dog who understands there are rules on leash.
  • A dog who can recover faster when the world gets exciting.
  • A dog you can actually take out into the world.

Why this works

Most walk problems are not just leash problems.

A lot of dogs know how to walk nicely when nothing is happening.

Then a dog appears. Or a squirrel. Or a person. Or a smell. Or the world gets exciting.

That is when the real problem shows up.

Your dog does not just need another cue. They need a better relationship to the leash, clearer rules outside, more useful engagement with you, and enough structure to know what to do when they want to do something else.

That is what we are building.

Who it is for

This is for you if...

  • Walks are the hardest part of your day.
  • Your dog pulls, lunges, barks, drags you, or checks out outside.
  • You feel embarrassed, tense, frustrated, or stuck on leash.
  • You want coaching, not just a pile of videos.
  • You are willing to do the work.

Who it is not for

This is not the right first step if...

  • Your dog has a serious bite history and you do not feel safe handling them.
  • Your dog is actively dangerous to people or animals.
  • You need a full behavior plan beyond leash walking.
  • You know you are not going to do the assignments.

If that sounds like your situation, book a free consult instead.
