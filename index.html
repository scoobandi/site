Trust to Train Relationship Reset

4 steps of behavior change

  1. 01

    Stop Negative Behaviors

    Unwanted behaviors are either self rewarding or extremely stressful. Either way, stop practicing the problem.

  2. 02

    Play With Your Dog

    • Build Confidence
    • Motivation
    • Emotional Control
    • Great Relationship
  3. 03

    Train Your Dog

    Use the previous foundation to teach your dog and practice the basic skills needed to navigate their world together.

  4. 04

    Live With Your Dog

    Use the skills you have built with your dog to navigate a safe, happy life together, solving problems as they arise.
