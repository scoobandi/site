02

The lens

The Trust to Train Relationship Reset.

Step 01 Stop the patterns making life harder

Sometimes the fastest progress starts with interrupting the cycle that keeps rehearsing the problem.

The plan has to work around walks, guests, kids, other dogs, routines, stress, and the real moments that have been hard.

Straight answers

Frequently asked questions.

Is this just a sales call?

No. The goal is to understand what is happening, help you make sense of it, and point you toward the right next step. If another option makes more sense, Joel will tell you.

What if I have already wasted money on training?

That does not mean your dog is hopeless or that you failed. A lot of people come here after spending money on advice that did not fit their dog, their home, or their life. The consult helps us stop guessing and look at the whole picture before you spend more.

What if my dog has bitten someone?

Start with a consult. Bite history, guarding, snapping, fighting, or unsafe household stress needs context before anyone gives you a plan.

Will one consult fix everything?

No. A consult gives you clarity, direction, and next steps. Some dogs need a few sessions, some need a bigger plan, and some are better served by a course or focused program.

Is this virtual or in person?

Both are possible. Appointments can happen in person in Sharpsburg, GA, or anywhere in the world through Zoom. Joel has successful 1-on-1 clients in 28 US states and 5 continents.
