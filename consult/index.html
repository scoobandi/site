Who this is best for

This call is probably a good fit if:

  • You are dealing with serious, confusing, or escalating behavior concerns
  • Your dog is showing reactivity, anxiety, aggression, or other more complex patterns
  • You want expert help figuring out the right level of support before spending money in the wrong place
  • You are open to private, virtual, or higher-support options if that is what your dog needs
  • You want clarity before committing to the wrong path

This may not be the best fit if:

  • You are only looking for free advice with no intention to take action
  • Your dog's issue is mild and clearly better suited for Digital Dog School
  • You are looking for a quick fix without consistency or follow-through
  • Your situation is outside the scope of what Joel helps with — in which case we will point you in the right direction
